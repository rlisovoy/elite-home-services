Common Questions

What applicants
ask us most.

Do I need prior cleaning experience?

Prior experience is preferred but not required. If you have the right work ethic and attitude, we provide fully paid training from day one to bring you up to our standard.

Will I work alone or with a team?

Depending on home size and service type, you may work solo or as part of a two-person team. You will always have support and a clear point of contact.

How soon can I start?

After your application is reviewed and an interview is completed, onboarding can begin within 1–2 weeks. Paid training begins on your first official day.

When does health insurance begin?

Full health insurance coverage begins after a 60-day introductory period for all full-time team members. We cover the full premium — no cost to you.

How is pay structured?

Team members are paid on a weekly basis. Pay is consistent and based on scheduled routes — not dependent on tips. Bonuses are awarded for exceptional client feedback.

What areas will I be working in?

We serve Highland Park, University Park, Southlake, Westlake, Colleyville, Plano, and Frisco. Routes are assigned to minimize your drive time between clients.
