Our Story

"I spent twenty-five years managing the finances of a Fortune 500 company. I decided it was time to bring that same standard to something closer to home — literally."
Rina Lisovoy
Founder & Owner, Elite Home Services
25 years in corporate finance. A lifelong passion for beautifully kept homes. Serving Highland Park, University Park, Southlake, Westlake, Colleyville, Plano & Frisco.

For twenty-five years, I lived by spreadsheets, quarterly reports, and back-to-back meetings. I built a career in finance that I was genuinely proud of — the discipline it demanded, the precision it required, the way a well-structured plan could bring order to chaos. But somewhere around year twenty-two, sitting in yet another budget review at 7 p.m. on a Thursday, I asked myself a question I had been quietly avoiding for a long time: Is this the thing I actually love?

The honest answer was no. And what surprised me most was how clearly I already knew what the answer was instead.

I have always been the person who finds genuine peace in a clean, beautifully kept space. Not just tidiness — I mean the kind of deep, intentional care that transforms a house into a sanctuary. While my colleagues unwound with golf or wine weekends, I would come home after a long week and spend Saturday morning making my home feel exactly right. The way a just-cleaned kitchen counter catches morning light. The satisfaction of every surface where it belongs. The quiet luxury of walking into a room that has been truly cared for. It never felt like a chore to me. It felt like restoration.

When I finally made the decision to leave corporate finance and start Elite Home Services, my colleagues thought I was having a midlife crisis. My family understood immediately. They had watched me pour that same meticulous attention into every home we ever lived in, and they knew — perhaps better than I did — that this was never really a detour. It was always where I was headed.

I brought everything from my finance career with me: the discipline to build systems that actually work, the understanding that trust is earned through consistency, the belief that a client relationship is only worth having if it's built on reliability and transparency. The pricing you see on this site is clear because I spent decades watching vague terms erode trust. The policies are fair because I know what it feels like to be a client who simply wants to know what to expect.

What I left behind was the distance. In finance, results live in numbers on a screen. Here, the result is the look on a client's face when they walk back into their home after we've been there — that exhale, that small moment of relief and delight. I get to witness that. Every single time, it reminds me that I made exactly the right decision.

Elite Home Services was born from a simple belief: that the homes of people who work hard and live fully deserve to be cared for with the same rigor and intentionality those people bring to everything else in their lives. I am not building the biggest cleaning company in DFW. I am building the one you will trust completely — and tell your neighbor about without hesitation.

Thank you for being here. I hope to have the honor of caring for your home.

— Rina Lisovoy
Founder & Owner, Elite Home Services
